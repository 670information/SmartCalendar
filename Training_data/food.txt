<item>
	<title>OUT for Work: Meet and Greet with LGBTQ Students</title>
	<description>	
	Need a break from studying? OUT for Work is coming to campus Tuesday, May 8 to meet with LGBTQ students! Free snacks and refreshments will be provided! See below for more information.

Riley Folds, the Executive Director of OUT for Work, will share with students information about OUT for Work and the resources available to them through the Career and Professional Development Center’s (CPDC) recent certification. The CPDC has recognized that LGBTQ students have unique career needs, and received certification in February 2012 as a career center that provides career information, tools, resources, and programs to LGBTQ students.

Riley will also be answering questions about students’ career development, including their resume writing, internship and job search, and how to locate inclusive work environments. OUT for Work is the only national non-profit organization dedicated to educating, preparing, and empowering LGBTQ college students for the workplace. Register for this event via TartanTRAK. RSVP not required to attend.
	</description>
